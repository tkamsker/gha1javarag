The business domain encompasses comprehensive management system for managing organizational structure with 3 core entities: **Areas, **Areas**, **Teams**, **Groups**, and **Users** and **Roles** with integrated **Messages**. This system supports administrative functions for managing organizational hierarchy, access control, communication, and workflow, and data flow management across organizational units.
